Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

What Slang Craft stores

Slang Craft stores all game data exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Discovered words and combinations (game progress)
  • Theme, sound, and view preferences (settings)
  • Save game slots
  • A first-visit flag (so the info popup only shows once)

What we do NOT collect

  • No cookies
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No personal information
  • No data sent to any server

Everything stays on your device. There is no backend, no database, and no third-party services.

Clearing your data

You can clear all Slang Craft data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site, or by using your browser's "Clear site data" option.
